The first 1000 STEP files from the ABC dataset (Koch et al., CVPR 2019), a collection of one million CAD models for geometric deep learning. Files are served gzip-compressed; decompress after downloading:

curl -sL <file-url> | gunzip > model.step

A machine-readable manifest with paths and sizes is at index.json. See the repository for details, bulk-download recipes, and attribution. The copyright of the CAD models is owned by their creators (Onshape Terms of Use 1.g.ii).
